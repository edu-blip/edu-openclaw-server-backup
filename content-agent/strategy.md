# Content Strategy — Q1/Q2 2026

_Last updated: 2026-03-04_

## Quarterly Goals
- **Primary**: More reach + ICP trust
- **Bucket mix**: Growth + Authority dominant, Conversion ≤20%

## Content Bucket Targets
| Bucket | Target % | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **Growth** | ~45% | Reach beyond niche — virality, followers, startup ecosystem broad appeal |
| **Authority** | ~35% | ICP trust — LinkedIn content, ghostwriting, B2B founder content strategy |
| **Conversion** | ≤20% | Case studies + CTA to book a call — use sparingly |

## What Each Bucket Means
- **Growth**: Resonates with the broad startup/founder world. Fundraising, hiring, AI trends, YC, company building, founder mindset, lifestyle trade-offs. If a founder who doesn't care about LinkedIn content would still find it interesting — it's Growth.
- **Authority**: Specific to Edu's niche (LinkedIn content strategy, ghostwriting, founder personal branding). Builds trust with Series A+ B2B founders who are Rethoric's ICP. Works well as paid promotion targeted at ICP.
- **Conversion**: Client results, case studies, "here's what we achieved for X" — direct pipeline generation. Max 20%.

## Paid Promo Notes
- Authority content → sponsor to ICP target audience (Series A+ B2B tech founders, raised >$10M, not healthcare)
- Growth content → organic reach is primary; promo less effective per dollar

- Q3 2026: revisit mix based on follower growth and pipeline numbers
