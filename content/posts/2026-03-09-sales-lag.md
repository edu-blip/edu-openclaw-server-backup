# Sales Lag

**Bucket:** Growth
**Hook type:** Specification Hook + Stakes Chain
**Status:** Draft — pending Edu approval
**Source:** Team check-in (2026-03-02)

---

Most founders quit their sales motion 3 weeks before it would have worked.

I did this. More than once.

Week 1: You send outreach. Crickets.
Week 2: A few opens. No replies.
Week 3: You start wondering if this channel even works.
Week 4: You kill it.

What you didn't see:

Week 5: The reply that was coming.
Week 6: The meeting that would've booked.
Week 8: The deal that would've closed.

Every sales motion has a built-in lag.

Outreach today becomes pipeline in 3 weeks.
Pipeline becomes closed revenue in 6.

The founders who win don't have a better playbook.

They just don't quit before the data comes back.
