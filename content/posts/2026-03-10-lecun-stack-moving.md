# Yann LeCun / The Stack Is Moving

**Bucket:** Growth
**Hook type:** Specification Hook
**Status:** Ready to post

Yann LeCun just raised $1.03B. Not for LLMs. For what comes after them.

My first thought wasn't "wow."

It was: how many founders are about to miss this?

Most of us are sprinting to build on top of GPT-4 architecture.

LeCun just bet a billion dollars that architecture is wrong.

World models don't predict text. They understand causality. Physics. How things actually work.

Not just the next token.

I've built 4 companies. Two got acquired. One I lost to a lawsuit.

Every time I got comfortable with the current stack, the stack moved.

This is the stack moving again.

The founders who win the next decade are already asking different questions.

Not "how do I build faster on this?"

"What comes after this?"

$1.03B says the AI era isn't settled.

It's barely started.
