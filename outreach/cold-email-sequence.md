# Cold Email Sequence — Rhetoric

**Target:** Series A+ B2B tech founders & C-level execs (raised >$10M, not healthcare)
**Spacing:** 3 days between each email
**Sending domain:** Use a separate domain (e.g., rhetoric-team.com) to protect primary domain reputation

---

## Email 1 — The Hook

**Subject:** Your LinkedIn is leaving money on the table

Hey {{first_name}},

Most Series A+ founders I talk to know they should be posting on LinkedIn — but between board meetings, hiring, and actually running the company, it never happens.

We ghostwrite thought leadership content for founders like you — 3 to 7 posts per week, all from a 30-min monthly interview. No extra time from you.

Worth a quick chat to see if it fits?

—Edu

## Email 2 — Social Proof

We don't just write content — we also turn post engagers into qualified prospect lists for your sales team, filtered to your ICP. Basically turns your LinkedIn into a lead gen channel.

## Email 3 — The Insight

**Subject:** Why your competitors are investing in LinkedIn right now

{{first_name}},

The founders winning right now aren't doing it themselves — they have a team making them look like a thought leader 5 days a week while they focus on building.

We handle all of it: strategy, ghostwriting, network growth, and weekly prospect lists from your post engagement.

15 minutes to see if we're a fit?

## Email 4 — The Breakup

**Subject:** Should I close the loop?

Hey {{first_name}},

I've reached out a couple times — totally understand if the timing isn't right or this isn't a priority.

If LinkedIn content is on your radar for this year, happy to chat whenever it makes sense. If not, no hard feelings — I'll stop filling your inbox.

—Edu

---

## Notes
- Personalize {{first_name}} and ideally the first line with a recent funding round, hire, or LinkedIn post (Clay enrichment)
- All emails under 100 words — respect their time
- Subject lines: no caps lock, no clickbait, conversational
- Send between 8-10am in their timezone (Tuesday-Thursday best)
