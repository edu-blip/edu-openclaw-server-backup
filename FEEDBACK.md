# FEEDBACK.md — Learning from Edu's Signals

Structured log of positive/negative feedback to identify patterns over time.
The nightly extraction cron reviews this and surfaces patterns monthly.

## How to log feedback

Automatic: I log it immediately when Edu reacts to or evaluates my output.
Manual: Edu can say "log that as a win" / "log that, that was off" at any time.

Signals I watch for:
- 👍 reaction or "good", "great", "perfect", "I liked that" → WIN
- 👎 reaction or "redo", "that's wrong", "not what I asked", silence + retry → MISS
- "remember this" / "always do it this way" → PREFERENCE (→ also update MEMORY.md)
